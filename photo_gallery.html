My Story


My journey into photography began in grade 10 when I joined the Specture Photography Club in the Public Relations department. It wasn't until I was elected to the 2021-2022 executive board that I revealed to my peers that I owned a Nikon Z6, which I hadn't realized was such a remarkable camera. Observing my friends' photography techniques piqued my interest, and I began learning by attending club events as a photographer and experimenting on my own. Moving to Canada provided a new source of inspiration, and I started capturing the beauty of the Canadian seasons, preserving those unique memories through my lens for the first time in my life.
